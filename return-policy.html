Return & Exchange Policy

At GlowHair Beauty, customer satisfaction is important to us. Due to the nature of wigs, hair extensions, lashes, and beauty products, we have specific return and exchange conditions to ensure hygiene and product quality.

1. Returns Eligibility

Returns are accepted within 7 days after receiving your order.

To qualify for a return:

  • The product must be unused and unworn
  • The lace must not be cut
  • The hair must not be washed, dyed, brushed, or altered
  • The product must be in its original packaging
  • Proof of purchase/order confirmation is required

2. Non-Returnable Items

For hygiene reasons, the following items cannot be returned:

  • Used wigs or hair products
  • Custom-made wigs
  • Lashes and makeup products that have been opened
  • Sale or discounted items

3. Damaged or Incorrect Orders

If you receive the wrong item or a damaged product, please contact us within 48 hours after delivery.

Kindly send:

  • Your order number
  • Clear photos/videos of the product
  • A short explanation of the issue

Once verified, we will arrange a replacement or refund where applicable.

4. Exchanges

Exchanges are allowed for eligible products depending on stock availability.

Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the mistake was from our side.

5. Refund Process

Approved refunds may take between 3–7 business days depending on your bank or payment provider.

6. Nationwide Delivery

We deliver across South Africa including:

  • Gauteng
  • Free State
  • Western Cape
  • KwaZulu-Natal
  • Eastern Cape
  • Limpopo
  • North West
  • Mpumalanga
  • Northern Cape

Delivery times may vary depending on location and courier services.
